1️⃣ Unsupported Audio Codec (Sabse common issue)

Kai IPTV streams me audio codec hota hai:

AC3
EAC3
DTS
AAC-LATM

Android ke kuch players inko decode nahi kar pate, isliye:

video chalti hai

audio mute ho jati hai

Example:

Video: H264
Audio: AC3

Solution:

Player me multiple audio engine support do.

Recommended players:

ExoPlayer
VLC Player

VLC almost sab codecs support karta hai.

2️⃣ Wrong Audio Track Selected

Kai channels me multiple audio tracks hoti hain.

Example:

Track 1 → English
Track 2 → Hindi
Track 3 → Commentary
Track 4 → Empty

Kabhi player automatically empty track select kar leta hai.

Result:

Video → OK
Audio → No sound

Solution:

Player me option add karo:

Audio Track Selection
3️⃣ IPTV Server Audio Bug

Kabhi stream me hi audio nahi hoti.

Example stream:

video.m3u8

Video segments me audio track missing hota hai.

Test ka tareeka:

Stream VLC player me chalao.

Agar VLC me bhi audio nahi:

Server issue
4️⃣ Hardware Decoder Bug

Android devices me kabhi hardware decoding audio ko break kar deta hai.

Especially:

cheap devices
old Android versions

Solution:

Player settings me option do:

Hardware Decoder
Software Decoder
5️⃣ Buffer / Network Issue

Low network speed me kabhi audio stream drop ho jati hai.

Symptoms:

Video OK
Audio missing

Fix:

Player me auto reconnect logic add karo.

6️⃣ ExoPlayer Audio Bug (Rare)

Kabhi ExoPlayer me issue hota hai.

Solution:

Stream reload:

player.stop()
player.prepare()
Best Solution (Professional IPTV Apps)

Professional IPTV apps ye 3 features use karte hain:

1. ExoPlayer
2. VLC fallback
3. Audio track switch

Flow:

If ExoPlayer audio fails
→ switch to VLC player
Prompt Replit Ko Dene Ke Liye

Fix audio issues in the OTTMEGA IPTV player.
